Music Record Information

My record collection started in my basement in 1995, where I began collecting vinyl records of my favorite music. I then started my store 5 years later to share my love of music with others who share similar interests.

Thats not to say albums are the only thing you can find in this establishment. You can also find posters, souvenirs, and other music-related items. Please come and share your love for music and others as well!

My record collection includes a variety of genres and artists so that anyone is more than welcome to come and explore.
