How to Ride a Bike

  1. Wear a helmet and safety gear.
  2. Adjust the bike seat and handlebars to fit your height.
  3. Find a flat, open area to practice.
  4. Get on the bike and place your feet on the ground.
  5. Start by scooting and balancing with your feet.
  6. Once balanced, place your feet on the pedals and start pedaling.
  7. Look ahead, not down, and steer gently.
  8. Practice stopping and starting safely.
  9. Keep practicing to improve your balance and control.
